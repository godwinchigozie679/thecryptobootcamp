What We're About

THE CRYPTO BOOTCAMP is a community where intending and prospective Cryptocurrency and Blockchain Enthusiasts can gain the required knowledge and skills to function and operate in the Cryptocurrency and Blockchain space, with special focus on educating the youths all over the Africa about the 4IR (4th Industrial Revolution); the Global Digital Economy; Business and Technology; Entrepreneurship; Acquisition of Digital Skills, and so much more.

THE CRYPTO BOOTCAMP serves as a trusted platform and community mandated to educate and train Young African Youths in the better understanding and capitalizing on this game-changer and transformative technology, called Blockchain and Cryptocurrency.

We are also a Community of Blockchain Advocates; who are interested in adopting, participating, supporting and starting up Solution Based Blockchain Projects; connecting the real world with the Blockchain space, through serious and strategic partnerships, and also building a community of active Cryptocurrency Traders.
